Discover Our Vegetables

Carrot

A root vegetable, usually orange, packed with Vitamin A. Great for eyesight!

Broccoli

A green plant from the cabbage family whose large flower heads are used as a vegetable.

Tomato

Botanically a fruit, but commonly used as a vegetable in cooking. Rich in lycopene.

Spinach

A leafy green vegetable loaded with nutrients like iron and vitamins.

Bell Pepper

Comes in various colors (red, yellow, green). Sweet and crunchy, high in Vitamin C.

Cucumber

A widely cultivated creeping vine plant bearing cucumiform fruits, used as vegetables.

Onion

A staple in many cuisines, adding flavor. Available in yellow, red, and white varieties.

Potato

A starchy tuber, versatile in cooking methods like boiling, baking, or frying.
